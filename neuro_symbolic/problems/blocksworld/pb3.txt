In the "Unstack 3" problem, the robot is presented with a simple vertical tower and tasked with dismantling it completely. This is a foundational exercise in automated planning because it requires the AI to understand physical dependencies—it cannot move a middle block until the top one is cleared.

Problem Breakdown

The Initial State: Three blocks are stacked in a single column. Block A is on top of B, and B is on top of C. Block C rests on the table.

The Goal: The robot must move the blocks so that A, B, and C are all sitting directly on the table, side-by-side, with nothing stacked on any of them.

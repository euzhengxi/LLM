In this scenario, you are tasked with moving eight individual blocks from a scattered initial state into a single, vertical stack. Here is the breakdown of the problem:

The Setup: 

Objects: There are 8 blocks, labeled a through h.

Initial State: All eight blocks are currently sitting directly on the table. Since nothing is stacked on top of them, every block is considered clear, and the robot arm is currently empty.

The Goal: The objective is to rearrange the blocks into a single 8-block high tower. The specific stacking order required is:

a on top of b

b on top of c

c on top of d

d on top of e

e on top of f

f on top of g

g on top of h

In this final configuration, block h will remain on the table, serving as the base of the tower, while block a will be at the very top.
